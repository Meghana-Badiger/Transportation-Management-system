Welcome to Presidency University Transportation System!

Our Services

We offer bus services for students and staff with multiple routes covering major locations. Our fleet is equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable journey.

Daily Bus Services ▼
Regular bus services for students and staff to major locations.
Shuttle Services ▼
Rajanukunte is the nearest point connecting the main road towards the city and other nearby localities. The university operates shuttle services for those who take public transportation from there.
Hostel Transport ▼
As the hostels are situated within a radius of two kilometers, the campus operates shuttle services along the route.

Each bus in the fleet is equipped with a first aid box, fire extinguisher, surveillance camera, GPS system, and speed governor. In addition to regular commuting, the buses are also used for industrial visits, excursions, and other short trips by students for academic purposes.

The university has employed a team of skilled and experienced drivers who were selected following a careful background investigation into their professional and behavioral traits.

Schedule

The transportation services are available from 6 AM to 7 PM, Monday through Saturday.

Timings for each route will be sent via email/SMS to the registered email ID/phone number.
